Firstly, we need to understand what is the nominal stress and net stress. **Nominal stress**: Stress calculated on the basis o fthe net cross section of a specimen without taking into account the effect of geometric discontinuities such as holes, grooves, fillets, etc. **Net stress**: Net stress is higher than nominal but not high enough. The discontinuity of the cross-section produces an increase of stress above $\alpha_n$.
