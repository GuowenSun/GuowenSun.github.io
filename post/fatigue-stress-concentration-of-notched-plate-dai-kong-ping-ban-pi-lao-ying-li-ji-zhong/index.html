Stress concentration of notched plate under axial loading 带孔平板在轴向拉力作用下应力集中

2021-04-01 #Stress concentration #Study #FEA

Recently, I did a coursework for the calculation of fatigue stress concentration of notched plate. In this blog, I will try to explain how to calculate the stress concentration factor and the value of maximum stress. The results will be compared with numerical results.

最近做了一个疲劳应力集中的作业，在这里记录一下如何计算带孔板的应力集中，并进行有限元验证。

The following picture displayed the plate with a middle hole (Figure 1). The material of plate is steel. It was under the tensile force with value of 1 kN. The mechanical properties was used as E = 210 Gpa, v =0.29. The detail parameter listed below:

  • Thickness: 1 mm
  • Length: 60 mm
  • Width: 10 mm
  • Diameter of hole: 5 mm

Firstly, we need to understand what is the nominal stress and net stress. **Nominal stress**: Stress calculated on the basis o fthe net cross section of a specimen without taking into account the effect of geometric discontinuities such as holes, grooves, fillets, etc.
**Net stress**: Net stress is higher than nominal but not high enough. The discontinuity of the cross-section produces an increase of stress above $\alpha_n$.

Analytical method

First, we need to calculate the nominal area:

A_n = (W - D) * t = (10 - 5) * 1 = 5 $mm^2$

The nominal stress will be:

αn=PAn=200MPa\alpha_n=\frac{P}{A_n} = 200 MPa αn​=An​P​=200MPa

The ratio between diameter of hole and width of plate is DW=0.5\frac{D}{W} = 0.5WD​=0.5. According to Figure 2, we can find the corresponding stress concentration factor is 2.19 approximately. The maximum stress will be around:

αm=Kt∗αn=438MPa\alpha_m = K_t * \alpha_n = 438 MPa αm​=Kt​∗αn​=438MPa
