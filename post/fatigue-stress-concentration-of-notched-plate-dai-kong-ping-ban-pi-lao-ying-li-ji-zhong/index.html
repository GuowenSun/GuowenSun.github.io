Recently, I did a coursework for the calculation of fatigue stress concentration of notched plate. In this blog, I will try to explain how to calculate the stress concentration factor and the value of maximum stress. The results will be compared with numerical results.

Firstly, we need to understand what is the nominal stress and net stress.
Nominal stress: Stress calculated on the basis o fthe net cross section of a specimen without taking into account the effect of geometric discontinuities such as holes, grooves, fillets, etc.

Net stress: Net stress is higher than nominal but not high enough. The discontinuity of the cross-section produces an increase of stress above σ_net.
